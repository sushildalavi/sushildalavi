## 👋 About Me

I’m **Sushil Dalavi**, an **AI Engineer / Research Assistant at the USC Norman Lear Center** and an **MS in Computer Science student at the University of Southern California**.

I build **AI-powered systems** across **RAG, NLP, LLM applications, information retrieval, data pipelines, and applied machine learning**. My work sits at the intersection of **research, engineering, and real-world deployment** — from large-scale multilingual media analysis pipelines to citation-grounded research assistants and healthcare AI systems.

- 🔭 Currently building **AI systems for research, media intelligence, and healthcare**
- 🧠 Interested in **RAG, LLM evaluation, retrieval systems, NLP, and applied ML**
- 🏫 Studying **MSCS at USC Viterbi**
- 🧪 Working on **multilingual AI pipelines at USC NLC**
- 📚 Building **ScholarRAG** for research paper discovery and synthesis
- 🎮 Also interested in **game development, AI in interactive systems, and full-stack product engineering**
