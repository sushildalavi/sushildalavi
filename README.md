# <img src="https://media.giphy.com/media/hvRJCLFzcasrR4ia7z/giphy.gif" width="28"> About Me

<img align="right" alt="coding" width="390" src="https://media.giphy.com/media/juua9i2c2fA0AIp2iq/giphy.gif" />

I’m **Sushil Dalavi**, an **AI Research Engineer / Research Assistant at the USC Norman Lear Center** and an **MS in Computer Science student at USC Viterbi**.

I build **AI-powered systems** spanning **RAG, LLMs, NLP, information retrieval, multilingual data pipelines, model evaluation, and full-stack AI products**. I’m especially interested in engineering systems that are not just technically strong, but also useful in real-world settings across **research, healthcare, and intelligent software applications**.

### What defines my work
- 🧠 Building **RAG, LLM, NLP, and retrieval systems**
- 🏗️ Designing **end-to-end AI products** from backend to frontend
- 🌍 Working on **multilingual AI pipelines** for research and media intelligence
- 📚 Building **ScholarRAG** for research discovery and synthesis
- 🏥 Exploring **healthcare AI systems** like MedSOAP
- 🎮 Also passionate about **game systems, stealth AI, and interactive experiences**
- 💼 Open to **SDE / SWE / AI/ML / Applied AI / Research Engineering roles**

# <img src="https://cdn-icons-png.flaticon.com/512/2620/2620971.png" width="24"> Signature Projects

### 📘 ScholarRAG — Retrieval-Augmented Research Assistant
A full-stack AI system for **research paper discovery, semantic retrieval, reranking, synthesis, and citation-grounded answers**.

**Highlights**
- Hybrid retrieval pipelines
- Semantic search + reranking
- Confidence-aware answer generation
- Citation-grounded outputs
- PDF ingestion and research workflow support

**Built With**  
`FastAPI` `React` `OpenAI` `pgvector` `FAISS` `MiniLM` `Postgres`

---

### 🧠 USC Norman Lear Center — Multilingual AI Research Pipelines
Building AI systems for **gender norms and media discourse analysis** across countries, platforms, and multilingual datasets.

**Highlights**
- Large-scale social media data workflows
- Embeddings and LLM-assisted thematic analysis
- Human + model evaluation pipelines
- Cross-country comparative research systems
- Research engineering for real-world social impact

**Built With**  
`Python` `AWS` `SageMaker` `Glue` `Embeddings` `LLMs` `Pandas`

---

### 🏥 MedSOAP — Clinical Documentation AI
An AI-driven healthcare system to transform **doctor-patient conversations into structured SOAP notes**.

**Highlights**
- Clinical dialogue understanding
- Structured note generation
- Fine-tuning workflows
- Model evaluation pipelines
- Healthcare-focused product engineering

**Built With**  
`Python` `Transformers` `LoRA` `Unsloth` `TRL` `Docker`

---

### 🎮 AI + Gameplay Systems

**Highlights**

- Gameplay analytics
- Horror and puzzle prototype development

**Built With**  
`Unity` `C#` `Gameplay AI` `Analytics`
