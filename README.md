## <img src="https://media.giphy.com/media/hvRJCLFzcasrR4ia7z/giphy.gif" width="26"> About Me

<img align="right" width="360" src="https://media.giphy.com/media/ZVik7pBtu9dNS/giphy.gif" alt="coding gif" />

I’m **Sushil Dalavi** — a graduate CS student at **USC** and an **AI Research Engineer / Research Assistant** at the **USC Norman Lear Center**.

My work sits at the intersection of **software engineering, AI/ML, and data systems**. I like building systems that are useful under real constraints: scalable backends, retrieval and search systems, data pipelines, model-powered products, and intelligent applications that can actually be deployed.

- 💼 Open to **SDE / SWE / AI/ML Engineer / Applied AI / Research Engineer** roles
- 🏗️ Strong interest in **backend engineering, APIs, system design, and ML-powered products**
- 🧠 Experience across **AI/ML, retrieval systems, NLP, data processing, and model evaluation**
- 📚 Building products like **ScholarRAG** and healthcare-facing AI systems
- 🌍 Interested in solving real-world problems with **engineering-first AI**
- 🎮 Also into **interactive systems, gameplay AI, and product design**
